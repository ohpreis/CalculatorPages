Annual Report

The annual report is a document that public corporations must disclose yearly to shareholders and regulators. The report describes the company's operations and financial condition over the previous year.

Annual reports often not only chronicle the previous year's activities and financials but often include forecasts about the company's plans and projections for the coming year.

Auditor

An auditor is a person or group of people officially authorized to review and verify a company's financial statements and ensures the company complies with local and federal tax laws.

Auditors protect a business by verifying there is no fraud or discrepancies in their accounting methods.
- Audited reports can be either qualified or unqualified.1.

Secured Lending

Secured Lending is when a borrower provides the lender with collateral to insure against a borrower defaulting on a loan. If the borrower defaults on the loan, the lender can seize the collateral to recoup the loss

Some loans are secured by their very nature. For example, a home mortgage or car loan is secured because the home or the car is the collateral.
